# AAS Capabilities Beacon Can Explain

## AI Agents
Assistants trained around business rules, FAQs, workflows, documents, and operating style.

## Workflow Automation
Intake, routing, reminders, follow-ups, summaries, task handoffs, approval queues.

## Internal Dashboards
Clean pages for leads, jobs, messages, notes, task status, approvals, bottlenecks.

## Knowledge Systems
Searchable internal knowledge bases for SOPs, policies, pricing, FAQs, procedures, and training.

## Document & Content Automation
SOPs, reports, training guides, customer reply drafts, proposals, service notes, checklists.

## Data Cleanup & Processing
Messy spreadsheets, duplicate records, PDFs, scanned forms, OCR, and scattered files turned into usable data.

## Example workflows
- Website lead -> AI organizes it -> owner gets clean review packet.
- Customer message -> AI summarizes and drafts reply for approval.
- Job notes -> quote notes, follow-up, or internal task.
- Staff asks private assistant questions about company procedures.
- Paper forms/PDFs -> structured searchable data.
- Owner gets morning briefing of leads, blockers, appointments, pending approvals.
